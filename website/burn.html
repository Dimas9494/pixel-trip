Burn to Evolve

Level up your trippers

Burn 2 identical characters to ascend to the next level. Each evolution unlocks a new form — the originals leave the chain forever.

→ →

Step 1 — Genesis → Awakened

+
BURN →

Send 2 identical Level 1 characters to the burn address. Receive 1 Level 2 tripper.

Step 2 — Awakened → Ascended

+
BURN →

Send 2 identical Level 2 characters to the burn address. Receive 1 Level 3 tripper.

  • Both burned NFTs must have the same character and the same evolution level.
  • If there are only two NFTs in a collection, burn both Stage 1 copies to immediately proceed to Stage 3 (skip Stage 2).
  • For example: Paired burn: 14 NFTs → 7 Stage 2 NFTs, then → 3 Stage 3 NFTs (odd numbers leave a remainder that will be modified later).
  • Burned tokens are permanently destroyed — only the evolved tripper remains.
